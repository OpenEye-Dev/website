About OpenEye

Growing out of a project at the MIT Media Lab, MIT Tata Center for Technology and Design and the Stanford Scalability Engineering Class, we think machine learning can dramatically increase health care access.

We build scalable technologies to enable open platforms for automatic disease understanding. We want to build knowledge of disease pathologies, understand trends and risk factors and accelerate existing screening pipelines.

Currently, we have contributors all throughout the world, with a small core team dedicated to improving our platform. Our collaborators include LV Prasad Eye Institute in Hyderabad, India and other large health providers.
